About Dr. Prachi

Dr. Prachi Katkar

Registration No: Reg. No. A-41869

Dr. Prachi Katkar is a highly experienced dental surgeon with over 10 years of clinical practice. She specializes in restorative and cosmetic dentistry and is known for her gentle, patient-focused approach. Having completed her BDS from a reputed university and various certifications in smile design, she’s committed to providing world-class dental care using the latest techniques and technology.

Her clinic reflects her belief that dentistry is not just science — it's an art that transforms lives through confident smiles.
